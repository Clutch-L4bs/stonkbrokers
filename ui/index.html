Collection Information

Stonk Brokers is a fully on-chain NFT collection. Each NFT holds an allocation of stock tokens in its token-bound wallet. At mainnet launch, holders will be able to burn their testnet Stonk Brokers to claim Robinhood mainnet Stonk Brokers. The collection includes a 5% royalty. These royalties are intended to be used to purchase stock tokens on Robinhood Chain and distribute them to Stonk Broker wallets as a monthly dividend.
