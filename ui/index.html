Collection Information

Stonk Brokers is a fully on-chain NFT collection, with each NFT holding an allocation of stock tokens in its token-bound wallet. On Robinhood mainnet, Stonk Brokers is intended to become the first NFT PFP collection with automated utility. The original 444 brokers and the expanded collection will merge into one unified mainnet collection. The project includes a 5% royalty, designed to buy stock tokens on Robinhood Chain and distribute them to Stonk Broker wallets as monthly dividends.
